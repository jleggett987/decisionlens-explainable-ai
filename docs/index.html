What DecisionLens is / is not

DecisionLens is

  • A decision reasoning walkthrough that makes tradeoffs explicit
  • An explainable recommendation with constraints, impacts, and uncertainty

DecisionLens is not

  • An autonomous decision-maker or “black box” optimizer
  • A legal compliance engine or a replacement for accountability
